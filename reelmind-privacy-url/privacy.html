Introduction

ReelMind ("we", "our", or "us") is an iOS app that lets you save, transcribe, and organize Instagram Reels via the iOS Share Sheet. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect when you use ReelMind, how we use it, and your rights over it.

By using ReelMind, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you have questions, you can always reach us at deepti.jain2810@gmail.com.

2

What Data We Collect

Your Account
  • Email address — required to create and sign in to your account
  • Display name — optional, set during sign-up or via Sign in with Apple / Google
  • User ID — a unique identifier assigned to your account when you register
Reels You Save
  • Instagram Reel URL — the link of the Reel you chose to save
  • Audio transcript — a text version of the Reel's audio, created by our processing pipeline
  • Caption and hashtags — the text content published with the Reel on Instagram
  • Creator handle — the username of the Instagram account that posted the Reel
  • Thumbnail image — a preview image of the Reel, fetched from Instagram
Automatically Collected
  • Push notification token — a device token used to send you save-confirmation notifications via Firebase Cloud Messaging
  • Basic app usage — session opens, screen views, and app interactions automatically recorded by Firebase Analytics (bundled in the app)
  • Device info — device model and iOS version, collected by Firebase Analytics
3

How We Use Your Data

  • Authentication — your email and user ID let you sign in and keep your library private
  • Processing your Reels — transcripts, captions, and hashtags are used to categorize and index your saved Reels so you can search and browse them
  • Push notifications — your device token is used solely to notify you when a Reel has been processed
  • App improvement — Firebase Analytics usage data helps us understand which features are used so we can improve the app
  • Customer support — if you contact us, we use your email to respond
We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not share your data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.
4

AI Processing & External Services

To power ReelMind's core features, we send portions of your Reel data to external services for processing. Here is exactly what each service receives and why:

Groq (Whisper)
Receives the audio file from your saved Reel to produce a text transcript. Groq processes the audio and returns the transcript — they do not retain your audio or transcript data beyond the duration of that request.
Groq (Llama 3.3)
Receives the transcript, caption, and hashtags from your Reel to determine the most appropriate category. This text is processed and the category result is returned — it is not stored by Groq.
Google Gemini
Receives the transcript, caption, and hashtags to create a semantic fingerprint (embedding) of your Reel. This fingerprint powers search and organization within your library. Google processes the text and returns the embedding — the raw text is not retained by Google.
Firebase (Google)
Used for push notifications (Firebase Cloud Messaging) and basic usage analytics (Firebase Analytics). Firebase collects app interaction data such as session opens and screen views, linked to your device.
Supabase
Stores your account information, saved Reels, transcripts, and all other app data in an encrypted, secure cloud database. Supabase is our primary data store.
Your consent: When you first use ReelMind, we will ask for your consent before any Reel data is processed by these services. You can withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account.
5

Data Retention

  • Account data and saved Reels — kept for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all of this data is permanently and immediately removed from our servers.
  • Firebase Analytics usage data — retained according to Google's standard Firebase Analytics retention period (up to 14 months by default), after which it is automatically deleted.
  • Push notification tokens — deleted when your account is deleted.

To delete your account and all associated data, go to Settings → Delete Account inside the ReelMind app. Deletion is immediate and permanent.

Your Rights

  • Access — you can view all your saved Reels and account information directly in the app
  • Deletion — delete your account and all associated data at any time via Settings → Delete Account
  • Analytics opt-out — to opt out of Firebase Analytics data collection, contact us at deepti.jain2810@gmail.com and we will disable it for your account
  • Questions or requests — for any privacy-related request, email us and we will respond within 48 hours
7

Security

  • All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Your data is stored in Supabase with encryption at rest and row-level security — only you can access your own Reels
  • Authentication is handled via Supabase Auth with industry-standard JWT tokens
  • We do not log or store passwords in plain text

While we take reasonable precautions, no system is completely immune to risk. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately.

8

Children's Privacy

ReelMind is designed for use alongside Instagram. Because Instagram requires users to meet their platform's minimum age requirements, you must be old enough to have an Instagram account in your country to use ReelMind.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children below Instagram's minimum age requirement. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has created a ReelMind account, please contact us at deepti.jain2810@gmail.com and we will delete the account promptly.
